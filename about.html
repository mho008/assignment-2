About The Gym

This 17,000sqft club on Level 8 and 9 of the prestigious Paragon Shopping Centre, is strategically located on the main Orchard Road shopping belt. This club has views of the city and a dramatic double-volume space design. The club boasts a large state-of-the-art gym with over 75 sets of Grade ‘A’ Technogym cardio equipment, that come with audio and visual entertainment and more than 40 pieces of weight machines.

It also has a fully equipped studio for Group Exercise classes, a cycling studio for group cycling classes and a dedicated Mind & Body studio for Yoga including Hot Yoga. Members can relax at a members' lounge with complimentary beverages, reading materials and computer terminals with complimentary internet access.

To get to us, we are a stone’s throw away from Orchard MRT Station and Somerset MRT Station on the North-South Line.
